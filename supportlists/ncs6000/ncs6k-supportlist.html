Cisco NCS6k MIB Support List

The following list indicates the MIBs that are supported by IOS XR on the NCS6k Router. SNMP version 1 MIBs are in the v1 directory and SNMP version 2 MIBs are in the v2 directory. Note that for almost every MIB .my in the v2 directory, there exists a SNMP version 1 conversion of the MIB -V1SMI.my in the v1 directory. The exception is SNMPv2-TC.my.
