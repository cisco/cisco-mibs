Cisco VPN3000 Concentrator MIB Support List

The lists below indicate which private MIBs are supported by the Cisco VPN3000 Concentrator. Depending on the version of the software, different MIBs are supported. In addition to the private mibs listed, the following RFC's are supported:

SNMP version 2 MIB files are in the v2 directory.
